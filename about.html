About Gradient Project

You can take any image, mask out the visible part of it, then create a background in the same shape of that image
From there you can do a variety of animations on it. Gradients are one example, but you could also make the background a ton of images as well. Or a pattern.

Created Oct 2025, by Shalev Widen with HTML, SCSS, and JS.
